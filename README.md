### Option 1 — Original Cases
Part 2 cases focus on real-world policy and business domains: Antibiotic Resistance, Hospital Visits & Mortality *(Spurious)*, Remote Work Productivity, Food Delivery & Restaurants, Class Size & Achievement, Technology in the Classroom, Social Media Algorithms, Password Policies & Breaches *(Spurious)*.

### Option 2 — Domain-Fit Science Cases
Part 2 cases are both domain-native AND grounded in natural/physical science: Antibiotic Resistance, Vaccines & Autism *(Spurious)*, Carbon Offsets & Climate, Organic Food & Pesticides *(Spurious)*, Sleep & Academic Performance, Sugar & Student Behavior *(Spurious)*, AI Bias in Medical Diagnosis, Screen Time & Brain Development *(Spurious)*.

### What's the same in both options
- Part 1 cases (Screen Time, Fast Fashion, School Start Times, AI Misinformation) with 3 curated evidence sources each
- Two-part assessment structure (Part 1: Evaluate, Part 2: Construct via CER)
- Competency ownership model, ES mapping, scoring weight options
- 6 evidence sources per Part 2 case, data tables, counterclaim requirement

## How to Use

Click any button in the nav bar to load that document. The interactive Student Briefs are fully functional — you can select cases, expand sections, and see all embedded evidence sources and data.
